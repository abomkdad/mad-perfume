1. How to request deletion

Send an email to our support team using the button above or write directly to the address below. Use the subject line MAD PERFUME account deletion request.

info@madperfume.co.il

2. Data we aim to delete

  • Customer profile details linked to the app account, such as name, optional email, preferred language, and marketing preferences.
  • Loyalty profile data linked to the customer account, including rewards QR and points balance where permitted.
  • App authentication access associated with the customer account.

3. Data we may retain

Some information may need to be retained even after an account deletion request. For example, order, refund, accounting, fraud-prevention, or legal records may be stored for the time required by applicable law or operational obligations.

4. What happens next

  • We review the request and verify that the requester controls the account.
  • We confirm whether any records must be retained for legal or transactional reasons.
  • We complete the deletion workflow and confirm closure through email.

5. Need general support instead?

For non-deletion support, visit /support.

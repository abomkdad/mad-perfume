Privacy and data use

Privacy policy for the MAD PERFUME mobile app

This page explains what the MAD PERFUME customer app collects, how the data is used to deliver orders and loyalty features, and how customers can contact us about privacy requests.

1. Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to authenticate customers, process orders, manage loyalty rewards, and provide support.

  • Account and sign-in data such as phone number, one-time-password verification, first name, last name, preferred language, and marketing consent.
  • Order data such as selected products, order number, fulfillment method, branch selection, delivery address reference, payment method, and order status history.
  • Loyalty data such as rewards QR code, available and pending points, tier, and redemption activity.
  • Support and operational data such as messages you send us and server logs used to keep the service secure.

2. Information we do not intentionally collect

The current project does not require precise GPS location, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, or advertising identifiers to place an order. The backend schema in this source project also does not store full payment card numbers.

3. How we use your information

  • To sign you in and protect your account.
  • To create, confirm, prepare, ship, and support your perfume orders.
  • To calculate loyalty points, rewards eligibility, and subscription benefits.
  • To localize the experience in Arabic, Hebrew, or English.
  • To answer support requests, prevent abuse, and improve reliability.
  • To send marketing messages only when you choose to receive them.

4. When we share information

We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate the app, such as hosting, messaging, customer support, payment, or order-fulfillment services. We may also disclose information when required by law, fraud prevention, tax, or accounting obligations.

5. Retention

We retain customer account and order information for as long as needed to operate the service, handle support, maintain loyalty history, and comply with legal, tax, fraud-prevention, and bookkeeping requirements.

6. Your choices

  • You can ask us to update your customer details if they are incorrect.
  • You can withdraw marketing consent at any time.
  • You can request deletion of your app account from the dedicated page at /delete-account.

7. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect customer information. No online system is perfectly secure, but we work to limit access to the data needed for legitimate business operations.

8. Contact us

For privacy questions, support, or data requests, email info@madperfume.co.il.

If app functionality or data practices change materially in later releases, this privacy policy should be reviewed and updated before release.
